Landlord Hive (llHive.com)
I built a platform for tenants to review landlords on a 5-point scale along three metrics (organization, communication, and ability to resolve tenant issues). The inspiration for this website is RateMyProfessors.com, and I thought that a similar platform would be incredibly helpful for people looking to assess the quality of their landlord before signing a lease.
